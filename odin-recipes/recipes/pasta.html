Pasta

Description

This is a recipe I was taught in college. I think it is safe to eat.

    Ingredients

  • Pasta(I prefer Penne)
  • Tomato Sauce(Cherry tomatoes as well if you like)
  • Fresh parsley
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian sausage
  • Three cloves of garlic
  • Half an onion

      • Directions

    1. Boil the pasta in water with a bit of salt for around 10-12 minutes(until it is soft)
    2. Take out the pasta water and save some pasta water in a bowl(around 2 cups) for later
    3. Cut the garlic and place it in the pot
    4. Take the sausage out of the wrapping and break it up to smaller pieces, then add some pepper
    5. Add the cherry tomatoes if available, and then the tomato sauce
    6. Stir often throughout
    7. Add some pasta water to make it more starchy
    8. Add some chopped parsley inside, and leave some for the end
    9. Add the pasta in, and stir
    10. Add the cheese whenever, and more pasta water if you feel like it
    11. Turn off the heat and add the parsley and stir
    12. Serve and enjoy!
