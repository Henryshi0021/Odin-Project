Lasagna

Description

This is one of the lasagnas in the world

There is no guarantee it is edible, but I tried my "best"

    Ingredients

  • Meat - Sweet Italian sausage and lean meat
  • Onion and Garlic - One onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomatoes - One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • SUGARR!
  • Spices and seasonings - Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasonings, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles(optional tbh)
  • Cheeses - Any one you prefer
  • Eggs - Sticky, but also eggs are always good, even in coffee!

      • Steps

    1. Make the sauce
    2. Cook the noodles(if you have any)
    3. Make the mixture
    4. Layer the noodles(optional) - Meat sauce, noodles(optional), mixture, cheese, meat sauce, more cheese, and REPEAT
    5. Cover with foil and bake for around 25 minutes covered with foil and 25 minutes uncovered
    6. Let it rest for 15 minutes
