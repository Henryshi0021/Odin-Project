Rice

Description

This is an image of rice. My favorite rice is Jasmine rice.

However, you can use any type of rice that you like

    Ingredients

  • rice
  • That one flake of parsley

      • Directions

    1. Take the rice of your choosing from the store and measure how much you eat
    2. Wash the rice several times with lukewarm water.

    3. Put it in the rice cooker and press the button and wait around 45 minutes to an hour. (No, you can not cook rice in five minutes)
    4. Add that one flake of parsley after you finished.

    5. Enjoy your rice!
